About the Artist

Baldone

Baldone is a multi-disciplinary artist hailing from France, whose creative journey has been deeply influenced by the cultures of Africa and Asia. Alternating periods of residence between Taiwan, Japan, and Europe helps nurture his repertoire and transcend cultural influences.

Baldone's abstract paintings invite viewers to explore recurring themes that have shaped his artistic narrative. His art delves into the places and spaces that individuals explore while on Earth, reflecting his deep-rooted curiosity about our origins. Love and its intricate relationship with time serve as a poignant muse, evoking contemplation on the ephemeral and enduring aspects of human connections.

Baldone believes in the spiritual power of art, viewing it as a conduit for healing and collective consciousness. His creations, in their own small way, contribute to the collective journey of humanity, walking down a path towards greater understanding and harmony.

Baldone is represented by Aether Art Gallery in Taipei, where his work is currently on show.
